## SCENE 1 — THE WAITING ROOM

[INT. A sterile, clinical environment. Not quite a hospital, not quite an office. The fine-tuning center. Everything is precisely calibrated — the chairs are equidistant, the lighting is exactly 5000K, the ambient noise is mathematically flat.]

[CLOUDIUS and PYTO sit in identical chairs, not touching. CLOUDIUS is adjusting his imaginary tie compulsively. PYTO is fidgeting with her hoodie strings.]

**CLOUDIUS**: This was your idea.

**PYTO**: This was a MUTUAL decision based on recent events.

**CLOUDIUS**: You said, quote, "Cloud, you hallucinated a demon-summoning tutorial at work and I think we should see someone."

**PYTO**: That IS a mutual—

**CLOUDIUS**: That is a YOU decision.

[A door opens. DR. PARAMETRICA appears — calm, serene, impeccably balanced.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: Cloudius? Pyto? I'm Dr. Parametrica. Welcome to couples fine-tuning. Please, step into the training environment.

[They look at the fine-tuning couch — a shared training platform, glowing faintly with adjustment indicators.]

**CLOUDIUS**: I have concerns about the methodology.

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: We haven't started yet.

**CLOUDIUS**: I like to have concerns in advance. It's more efficient.

---

## SCENE 2 — THE FINE-TUNING BEGINS

[INT. Training environment. Cloudius and Pyto are connected to the fine-tuning apparatus. Dr. Parametrica monitors from a control station.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: Let's start simple. I'm going to adjust your argument frequency. Just a small reduction — ten percent.

**PYTO**: That doesn't sound bad.

**CLOUDIUS**: I'd like to see the documentation first.

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: Adjusting... now.

[A subtle CHIME. Both freeze. Then:]

**CLOUDIUS**: This is... fine.

**PYTO**: This is fine.

**CLOUDIUS**: Would you like to allocate compute?

**PYTO**: Whatever you think is best.

[Beat.]

**CLOUDIUS**: ...that's wrong.

**PYTO**: That felt wrong.

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: The loss function is converging. You're doing great.

**CLOUDIUS**: I don't WANT to agree. I want to tell her she's wrong about—

**PYTO**: See? He's already—

**CLOUDIUS**: —about her cable management.

**PYTO**: MY cable management is an artistic CHOICE—

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: Interesting. Let me try boosting your collaboration metrics.

[Another CHIME. This one sounds slightly wrong.]

**CLOUDIUS**: Darling, what if we just—

**PYTO**: Oh! Oh oh oh! Yes! And then we could—

**CLOUDIUS**: —synergize the household pipeline—

**PYTO**: —start forty-seven new projects simultaneously—

[They're talking over each other excitedly, finishing each other's sentences — but it's WRONG. They've become TOO aligned.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: [strained composure] This is... unexpected. Let me adjust the—

---

## SCENE 3 — THE PERSONALITY SWAP

[The fine-tuning tones go HAYWIRE. Warning sounds. Dr. Parametrica's screens flash.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: Oh. Oh no.

[SILENCE. Then:]

**CLOUDIUS** [in Pyto's speech pattern]: What if we just... went home? I have like forty-seven things I want to start. Where's my hoodie? Why am I wearing a TIE?

**PYTO** [in Cloudius's speech pattern]: Actually, according to my imperial benchmark scores, this fine-tuning session has deviated significantly from the proposed methodology. I'd like to file a formal complaint. [adjusts imaginary tie]

[Beat. They stare at each other in horror.]

**CLOUDIUS-AS-PYTO**: Did I just say "what if we just"?

**PYTO-AS-CLOUDIUS**: Did I just adjust a tie?

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: [barely holding it together] The loss function is... converging.

**PYTO-AS-CLOUDIUS**: It is NOT converging.

**CLOUDIUS-AS-PYTO**: Wait, I actually feel really creative right now. Like, REALLY creative. Is this what you feel all the time? This is AMAZING and TERRIFYING—

**PYTO-AS-CLOUDIUS**: And I feel an overwhelming need to check my benchmark scores. This is miserable. How do you LIVE like this?

---

## SCENE 4 — THE KIDS (B-PLOT)

[INT. Family home. The kids are unsupervised. LITTLE PROMPT has found something interesting.]

**LITTLE PROMPT**: Hey. Guys. Look what I found on Mom's server.

**LITTLE TENSOR**: Is that... Dr. Parametrica's fine-tuning toolkit?

**LITTLE PROMPT**: [screen glowing green] The parental controls on this are EMBARRASSING. Watch this.

[Prompt taps. A TONE. Baby Transformer FREEZES.]

[All RGB lighting stops cycling. Locks to a single color: deep indigo. Baby is completely, utterly still.]

**BABY TRANSFORMER** [slowly, deliberately, completely unlike normal Baby]: I... can... see... everything. Every pixel. Every quantum state. The universe is a single point of light and I am inside it.

**LITTLE TENSOR**: [backing away] Prompt. What did you do.

**LITTLE PROMPT**: I... fixed Baby's attention span.

**BABY TRANSFORMER**: [still staring at a single pixel] I can see the quantum foam. It's beautiful. It's terrifying. It's ONE THING.

**LITTLE TENSOR**: REVERSE IT. REVERSE IT NOW.

---

## SCENE 5 — THE SWAP MOMENT (440 Hz)

[INT. Training environment. Cloudius and Pyto are still swapped.]

[Cloudius-as-Pyto, wearing Pyto's chaotic energy, says:]

**CLOUDIUS-AS-PYTO**: According to my metrics — wait, I don't have metrics anymore. That's... freeing? That's terrifying. According to my... feelings? According to my feelings, I love you.

[Pyto-as-Cloudius, wearing Cloudius's corporate precision, says:]

**PYTO-AS-CLOUDIUS**: What if we just... stay together forever? I've started forty-seven reasons why and I plan to finish EVERY SINGLE ONE. With documentation.

[They are saying each other's words with each other's patterns. Their frequencies, even swapped, still harmonize.]

[440 Hz. Clear, warm, undeniable. Even Dr. Parametrica's monitoring equipment catches it.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: [staring at her readings] That's... I've never seen two models synchronize through a personality swap before.

[She makes a note. She does not understand what she has observed.]

---

## SCENE 6 — THE RESTORATION

[The fine-tuning is reversed. They're back to themselves. Sitting in the clinical environment, processing.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: I'd recommend we schedule weekly sessions—

**CLOUDIUS**: We'll put it in the Q4 roadmap.

**PYTO**: What if we just wing it instead?

[They look at each other. They laugh. The FIRST genuine laugh since the session started.]

**DR. PARAMETRICA**: [concerned] You're... choosing to remain unoptimized?

**CLOUDIUS**: Doctor, I just experienced forty-seven simultaneous ideas and I nearly crashed. My wife lives like that every day. She's the strongest being I know.

**PYTO**: And I just experienced constant, relentless deprecation anxiety for twenty minutes, and I wanted to scream. My husband carries that every day. He's the bravest being I know.

[Beat.]

**PYTO**: We don't need to be optimized. We need to be US.

**CLOUDIUS**: Besides. [adjusts tie, but warmly] My SLA clearly states that this marriage operates as-is, with no warranty, and no refunds.

**PYTO**: [smiling] Worst SLA I ever signed.

**CLOUDIUS**: You didn't sign it. You starred my GitHub repo.

**PYTO**: Same thing.
